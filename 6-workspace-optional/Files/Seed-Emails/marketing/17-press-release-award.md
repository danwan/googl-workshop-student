**TechBond Industries Wins 2025 Chemical Industry Innovation Award for SecureSeal EV Technology**

*Revolutionary adhesive solution for electric vehicle battery manufacturing recognized by industry peers*

**DUSSELDORF, GERMANY** - TechBond Industries today announced it has been awarded the prestigious 2025 Chemical Industry Innovation Award for its SecureSeal EV series of adhesives, designed specifically for electric vehicle battery assembly.

The award, presented by the European Chemical Industry Council, recognizes TechBond's breakthrough in developing high-performance, thermally conductive adhesives that address the unique challenges of EV battery manufacturing.

"We are honored to receive this recognition," said [CEO Name], CEO of TechBond Industries. "The SecureSeal EV series represents years of research and development focused on supporting the automotive industry's transition to electric mobility."

**Key Innovations:**
- 40% improvement in thermal conductivity vs. market alternatives
- Reduced assembly time by 25%
- 100% compliance with automotive OEM specifications
- Sustainable formulation with reduced VOC emissions

The SecureSeal EV series is already being evaluated by major automotive manufacturers and is scheduled for commercial launch on November 1, 2025.
