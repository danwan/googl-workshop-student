Hi Brand Team,

Here are the final results from our GlossStyle Professional Q3 campaign.

**Campaign Overview:**
- **Duration**: July 1 - September 30
- **Target**: Professional hair stylists and salons
- **Budget**: €180K
- **Channels**: Digital ads, trade publications, salon partnerships

**Results:**

| Metric | Target | Actual | Performance |
|--------|--------|--------|-------------|
| Impressions | 5M | 6.2M | 124% |
| Click-through rate | 2.0% | 2.4% | 120% |
| Lead generation | 800 | 1,050 | 131% |
| Salon sign-ups | 150 | 187 | 125% |
| Cost per lead | €225 | €171 | 132% (under budget) |

**Top Performing Content:**
1. "Transform Your Salon" video - 2.1M views
2. Before/after Instagram carousel - 89K engagements
3. Professional tips blog series - 45K unique visitors

**Key Learnings:**
- Video content significantly outperformed static images
- Instagram Stories drove highest engagement with salon owners
- Trade publication ads had lower ROI than digital

**Recommendations for Q4:**
- Increase video budget by 30%
- Launch TikTok presence for younger stylists
- Partner with 3 additional influencer stylists
