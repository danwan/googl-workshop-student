Team,

Here are the A/B test results from our October email campaigns.

**Test 1: Subject Line Testing**
Campaign: Monthly Newsletter

| Version | Subject Line | Open Rate |
|---------|--------------|-----------|
| A | "TechBond October Newsletter" | 22.3% |
| B | "5 Adhesive Tips That Could Save You Thousands" | 34.7% |
| **Winner** | Version B | **+55% improvement** |

**Test 2: CTA Button Color**
Campaign: SecureSeal Teaser

| Version | Button Color | Click Rate |
|---------|--------------|------------|
| A | Blue (brand color) | 4.2% |
| B | Orange (contrast) | 5.8% |
| **Winner** | Version B | **+38% improvement** |

**Test 3: Send Time**
Campaign: Product Update

| Version | Send Time | Open Rate |
|---------|-----------|-----------|
| A | Tuesday 9 AM | 28.1% |
| B | Thursday 2 PM | 31.4% |
| **Winner** | Version B | **+12% improvement** |

**Overall Campaign Performance:**

| Metric | September | October | Change |
|--------|-----------|---------|--------|
| Avg Open Rate | 24.5% | 29.8% | +22% |
| Avg Click Rate | 3.8% | 5.1% | +34% |
| Unsubscribe Rate | 0.4% | 0.3% | -25% |

**Recommendations for November:**
1. Use benefit-driven subject lines (not generic)
2. Test orange CTAs on promotional emails
3. Schedule key campaigns for Thursday afternoons
