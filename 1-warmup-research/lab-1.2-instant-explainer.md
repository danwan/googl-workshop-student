# 🟢 Lab 1.2 — The Instant Explainer

> **Your mission:** Turn three technical TechBond documents into a slick **AI-narrated video** and a clickable **mind map** — the fastest "explain this to me" button ever invented. 🎬🗺️

| 🏆 Level | ⏱️ Time | 🧰 Builder | 📦 Data you need |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 · Easy | ~15 min | **NotebookLM** (Studio → Video Overview + Mind Map) | Upload `BondMax-500-Technical-Data-Sheet.pdf`, `Adhesive-Application-Guide-Automotive.pdf`, `Quality-Management-Handbook.pdf` |

---

## 🎬 The story

A brand-new hire just joined the **TechBond** technical team. 🧑‍🔬 They need to learn three things fast: the flagship **BondMax 500** specs, how to actually *apply* the adhesive on automotive jobs, and the company's quality rules. That's a thick stack of PDFs and a very glazed-over expression. 😵‍💫

Instead of handing them homework, you'll build them a 2-minute narrated **video explainer** and an interactive **mind map** they can click around. Onboarding, done in the time it takes to get coffee. ☕ Let's build the explainer. 🚀

---

## 🛠️ Build it (follow along)

**Step 1 — New notebook, fresh start.**
Go to **[notebooklm.google.com](https://notebooklm.google.com)** → **+ Create new**. (Keep this separate from Lab 1.1 so your sources stay clean.)

**Step 2 — Add the 3 technical sources.**
Click **+ Add source** and upload:
- `BondMax-500-Technical-Data-Sheet.pdf`
- `Adhesive-Application-Guide-Automotive.pdf`
- `Quality-Management-Handbook.pdf`

> 💡 Mixing specs + how-to + quality rules gives NotebookLM a full picture — so the explainer connects *what the product is* to *how to use it correctly*.

**Step 3 — Open Studio → Video Overview. ⭐ (the brand-new toy)**
In the **Studio** panel, click the **Video Overview** tile. This is NotebookLM's newest trick: it builds **AI-narrated slides** with visuals pulled from *your* sources — basically an auto-generated explainer video.

> 🎚️ Optional steer: *"Create an onboarding explainer for a new TechBond technician. Cover BondMax 500's key specs, the correct automotive application steps, and the most important quality rules. Keep it clear and beginner-friendly."*

**Step 4 — Generate and watch. 🍿**
Click **Generate** and wait a minute or two while it builds the slides and narration. ⏳ When it's ready, press **▶ Play**. Watch the AI walk through BondMax 500 like a polished training video — narration, visuals, the works.

**Step 5 — Now the Mind Map. 🗺️**
Back in **Studio**, click the **Mind Map** tile. NotebookLM draws a branching map of how every concept in your sources connects — product, application, quality, all linked.

**Step 6 — Click around and explore.**
**Click the nodes** to expand branches. Follow a thread like *BondMax 500 → application surface prep → quality checks*. Each node zooms you into a sub-topic, and you can ask the chat about anything you click. It's the "see how it all fits together" view. 🤯

## 💡 Stuck? Open a hint

<details>
<summary><strong>Hint 1 — I don't see Video Overview</strong></summary>

It lives in the **Studio** panel on the right, alongside Audio Overview, Mind Map, and Reports. If a tile is greyed out, your sources may still be importing — give it a few seconds. Video Overviews can take a little longer to generate than audio, so be patient. ⏳
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Hint 2 — The video is too long / too generic</strong></summary>

Re-generate and use the customize box to tighten the focus:
> *"Keep it under 3 minutes. Focus only on the 5 things a new technician must know before their first BondMax 500 job."*

The more specific you are, the sharper the explainer.
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Hint 3 — How do I get the most out of the Mind Map?</strong></summary>

Don't just stare at it — **click a node**, then read the linked passage, then click the chat to ask *"Explain this branch in simple terms."* The map is a navigation tool: it shows you *where* to dig, and the chat digs for you (with citations).
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>✅ Show me a full solution</strong></summary>

1. **+ Create new** → new notebook.
2. **+ Add source** → upload the BondMax data sheet, the automotive application guide, and the Quality Management Handbook.
3. **Studio → Video Overview** → (optional) paste the onboarding prompt from Step 3 → **Generate** → **▶ Play** and watch.
4. **Studio → Mind Map** → click nodes to explore how product, application, and quality connect.
5. (Bonus) **Studio → Reports → Study guide** → generate a new-hire study guide.

**Done — onboarding material that built itself.** 🎓
