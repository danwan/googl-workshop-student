# Module 1 · Warm-Up — NotebookLM Research Lab 🔬

> **Workshop role:** Day 1 core with Lab 1.1. Day 2 core for newcomers, choice for returning participants, and bonus for remaining labs.

Meet **NotebookLM** — your new AI study buddy. 🤓 You hand it a pile of boring documents, and it hands you back a chatty **podcast** you'd actually enjoy, a narrated **video explainer**, a clickable **mind map**, or a tidy **briefing doc** — all grounded in *your* sources, with **citations** so you can trust every word. It's like having the world's fastest research assistant who read everything for you and is ready to talk about it. 🎧🧠

Why is this the perfect warm-up? Because there's **zero pressure** and **nothing to break**. No agents to design, no prompts to perfect, no logic to wire up. You'll just play, listen, and explore — and by the end you'll *feel* how good AI is at digesting documents. That instinct pays off in every later module.

> 🟢 **No connectors needed.** You just upload files or paste links. That's the whole setup.
>
> 🌐 Open **[https://notebooklm.google.com](https://notebooklm.google.com)** and sign in. This remains the workshop's canonical route. If Lab 0.1 enabled the integration, **Agents → Made by Google → NotebookLM** opens the same project's notebooks inside Gemini Enterprise. The PDFs live in this module's `Files/` folder.

## 🧰 What you'll touch in NotebookLM

- **Sources** — upload PDFs, paste website URLs or YouTube links, or drop in raw text.
- **Studio panel** — four magic tiles: **Audio Overview**, **Video Overview**, **Mind Map**, **Reports**.
- **Chat** — ask anything about your sources; every answer comes with **citations**.
