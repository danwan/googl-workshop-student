# 🟢 Lab 1.1 — The Boardroom Podcast

> **Your mission:** Take 3 dense business reports nobody wants to read, and turn them into a lively podcast you'd actually listen to on your commute — then *talk back to the hosts live*. 🎙️

| 🏆 Level | ⏱️ Time | 🧰 Builder | 📦 Data you need |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 · Easy | ~15 min | **NotebookLM** (Studio → Audio Overview) | Upload `Q3-2025-Sales-Report-EMEA.pdf`, `Annual-Business-Review-2025.pdf`, `SecureSeal-EV-Series-Launch-Plan.pdf` |

---

## 🎬 The story

It's Monday at **TechBond Industries**. ☕ The leadership team just dropped three monster reports in your lap: the EMEA Q3 sales numbers, the full annual business review, and the launch plan for the hot new **SecureSeal EV** adhesive. Reading all three? That's your whole morning gone. 😴

So you're going to cheat — the *smart* way. You'll feed all three to NotebookLM and let two AI hosts turn them into a punchy podcast that hits the highlights. Then, when the hosts say something juicy, you'll **interrupt them live** and ask a follow-up. Press record. 🚀

---

## 🛠️ Build it (follow along)

**Step 1 — Make a notebook.**
Go to **[notebooklm.google.com](https://notebooklm.google.com)** and click **+ Create new** (or **New notebook**). An empty notebook opens, asking for sources. Think of a notebook as a folder that only knows what *you* put in it.

**Step 2 — Add your 3 reports (the magic step 🪄).**
Click **+ Add source** and upload all three PDFs:
- `Q3-2025-Sales-Report-EMEA.pdf`
- `Annual-Business-Review-2025.pdf`
- `SecureSeal-EV-Series-Launch-Plan.pdf`

> 💡 No connectors, no setup — you're just *handing it files*. NotebookLM reads every page and now "knows" all three reports.

**Step 3 — Open the Studio panel.**
On the right, find the **Studio** panel. You'll see four tiles: **Audio Overview**, **Video Overview**, **Mind Map**, and **Reports**. Today's star is **Audio Overview**. 🎧

**Step 4 — Pick the Deep Dive format.**
Click **Audio Overview**. Before you generate, choose the format — pick **Deep Dive**. (That's the classic two-host show where they chat through your sources like co-hosts on a podcast.)

> 🎚️ Optional: there's a "customize" box where you can steer the hosts — e.g. *"Focus on the SecureSeal EV launch and the EMEA numbers; keep it upbeat and under 10 minutes."* Totally fine to skip it for now.

**Step 5 — Generate, then listen.**
Click **Generate**. Grab water — it takes a minute or two to produce. ⏳ When the player appears, hit **▶ Play**. You'll hear two AI hosts banter through your TechBond reports, connecting the sales results to the EV launch. Surprisingly good, right? 😄

**Step 6 — Talk to the hosts LIVE (the wow moment 🤩).**
Click **Interactive mode** (a "Join"/"Interactive" button on the audio player). Now you can *interrupt the show* and ask a question out loud — the hosts will stop and answer you using your sources. Try asking:

> *"What's the biggest risk to the SecureSeal EV launch?"*

Listen as they pull the answer straight from your launch plan. You just had a conversation with a podcast. 🎉

## 💡 Stuck? Open a hint

<details>
<summary><strong>Hint 1 — I don't see the Audio Overview option</strong></summary>

Make sure at least one source uploaded successfully (you should see your PDFs listed on the left). The **Studio** panel is on the right side of the notebook. If the tiles look greyed out, your sources are probably still importing — wait a few seconds and refresh.
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Hint 2 — Where is "Interactive mode"?</strong></summary>

You'll only see it **after** the Audio Overview finishes generating. Look on or beside the audio player for a button like **Interactive mode** / **Join**. Click it, then speak (or type) your question. The hosts pause and respond live.
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Hint 3 — Make the podcast more focused</strong></summary>

When you click Audio Overview, use the **customize** box before generating:
> *"You are briefing TechBond's leadership. Spend the most time on the SecureSeal EV launch plan and the EMEA Q3 results. Highlight risks and opportunities. Keep it energetic and under 10 minutes."*
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>✅ Show me a full solution</strong></summary>

1. **+ Create new** → new notebook.
2. **+ Add source** → upload the 3 PDFs (Q3 EMEA, Annual Business Review, SecureSeal EV Launch Plan).
3. In **Studio**, click **Audio Overview** → choose **Deep Dive**.
4. (Optional) paste the focus prompt from Hint 3 → **Generate** → wait ~1–2 min.
5. Press **▶ Play** and listen.
6. Click **Interactive mode** → ask *"What's the biggest risk to the SecureSeal EV launch?"* → the hosts answer from your launch-plan source.

**Done — three reports became a podcast you can actually talk to.** 🎙️
</details>
